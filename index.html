Our Suggestions

Chania

Chania a place where different civilizations have flourished throughout the centuries.Strolling around the Old Town's maze-like alleys with the beautiful Venetian mansions, the fountains and the churches will guide you through well-preserved historical monuments.

EXPLORE
Milos

The volcanic activity in ancient years has endowed Milos island with an exciting variety of gorgeous landscapes, consequently offering the visitor a wide range of activities. The funny shapes of the rocks and their wonderful colours at the beautiful white sandy beaches are one expression of the volcanic features of Milos.

EXPLORE
Meteora

Kalabaka is built on the feet of the Meteora and it is at the left side of the river Pinios at the point that it enters the plain of Thessaly. Internationally known due to the famous Monasteries on the top of the Meteora rocks, suspended in the air.

ENDLESS COASTLINES

With almost 16.000km of coastlines, 6.000 islands and islets, Greece has one of the longest coastlines in the world. Thousands of beaches are available to the visitors. Discover yours!
